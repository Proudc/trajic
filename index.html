Synopsis

Trajic is a program for compressing GPS trajectory data. To find out more about how the system works and see some experimental results, check out the official research paper.

Usage

Compress a trajectory losslessly:

trajic c traj.plt

Compress a trajectory with max errors of 0.1 s temporally and 0.001 degrees spatially:

trajic c traj.plt 0.1 0.001

Decompress a trajectory:

trajic d traj.tjc
